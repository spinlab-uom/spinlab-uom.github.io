New paper: Temporal trends in eating disorder and self-harm incidence rates among adolescents and young adults in the UK

June 20, 2023

In our latest paper we examine the incidence rates of these psychopathologies among young people in the UK in the 2 years following onset of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Temporal trends in eating disorder and self-harm incidence rates among adolescents and young adults in the UK in the 2 years since onset of the COVID-19 pandemic: a populationbased study Trafford AM, Carr MJ, Ashcroft DM, et al. The Lancet Child & Adolescent Health, DOI: 10.1016/S2352-4642(23)00126-8.
